Deploying your website has never been easier. With LaunchDrop, you just place the files for your site in your Dropbox, and we do the rest.

If you have a dropbox folder with your site's files in it, you're ready to go! Just link your Dropbox to LaunchDrop, and your site will get served without any unnecessary work. Once your site is live, it is automatically updated when your Dropbox gets synced.
